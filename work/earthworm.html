This is a recreation of my favourite level in Earthworm Jim 2. Psy-Crow is in Peter Puppy's world and he has taken Pete's 600 family members hostage, and he's throwing the helpless puppies out of the window to get rid of the evidence. Jim must guide the puppies to Peter with the giant marshmallow o' love.
Some of the graphics were extracted from the original SNES game by me and some are taken from spriters-resource.com. The game was made with Sprite-Kit for iOS 7.0 and written in Objective-C.
