FatMama is a character I've developed during a game design course.
It featured the full process of creating a video game character: At first the initial Idea was developed and concepts were drawn. References were gathered and the character was blocked out using Z-Spheres. A high-poly mesh was created in ZBrush. To get a game-ready, low-poly mesh the high-poly model was remeshed and uv'd in 3D-Coat. Color and normal-maps were generated in XNormal. The Character was then rigged and animated using keyframe-animation in Maya. In addition, a small game was created around the character in Unity 3D. All other models in this game were also made by me.
FatMama dreams of a world of sweets while being very hungry. She can't eat things that are bigger than her otherwise she'll get hurt.
